NPC 1 and Echo puzzle dialogue

NPC: Oh hi, I noticed something strange with the writing on this gravestone, it doesn't say a death date or anything. But its some sort of story.

NPC: Here I'll read it to you. 

Riddle contents:

In the cold restless sea
The Bow kept sturdy as can be
But pushed back the Bow was
For a mighy wave against the front struck it does

Starboard filled to the brim
Bailed out east, but it looked grim. 

The Port bombarded by waves to harm
My body flung until I broke my right arm 

As the ocean engulfs the Stern below
I can see the stars, so bright yellow




Solution:

The story is about the ship captain sailing the rough seas. The Bow, Starboard, Port, and Stern indicate the parts of a ship. The Bow is the front of the ship, Starboard is right, Port is left, and Stern is back if the person is looking forward. These corrospond with how the gravestones are positions. This allows the player to know which one to interact with. Interacting with a gravestones tilts it forwards, backwards, left and right. Based off the sections that rhyme together, the player can deduce which way to tilt the gravestones

Bow = cross
cross is tilted back

Starboard = right gravestone
gravestone is tilted to the right

Port = left gravestone
gravestone is tilted to the right

Stern = back gravestone
gravestone is tilte backwards
